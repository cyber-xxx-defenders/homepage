Navigating Cybersecurity challenges as a Public Good Organization!

Join us for an evening of discussion on the unique cybersecurity challenges facing Non-Profit, B-Corps, and Municipal organizations. Cyber Defenders and our partners will highlight the most common threats and various tools, strategies, and expertise available. Food will be provided by our host, the Airbnb Information Security CSIRT team.

Proposed Agenda

  • 6:00-6:30 pm - Arrival, registration, networking.
  • 6:30-7:00 pm - Introductory presentation on unique cybersecurity challenges facing public good orgs. These topics will cover highlights from 15-minute interviews with attendees.
  • 7:00-7:30 pm - Presentation on tools and strategies for public good organizations.
  • 7:30-8:00 pm - A collaborative problem solving design session on cybersecurity for public good orgs.

  • 8:30-9:00 pm - Networking
